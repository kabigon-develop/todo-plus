# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Todo Plus is for individual productivity users, especially developers and solo operators who need one place to manage tasks, capture ideas, move ideas toward execution, and review monthly activity. They use it during focused work sessions, quick planning passes, and end-of-month reflection.

## Product Purpose

Todo Plus helps users turn scattered work into a manageable local system: todos for concrete execution, an idea board for early thoughts, and a dashboard for activity patterns. Success means users can quickly capture, triage, complete, convert, and review work without needing a remote account or collaboration setup.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, clear, efficient.

The interface should feel like a dependable workbench: calm enough for repeated daily use, explicit enough to scan quickly, and restrained enough that the user's work remains the focus.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, oversized hero treatment, decorative card grids, excessive gradients, noisy gamification, and UI that feels like a template SaaS landing page. Avoid burying routine actions behind modal-heavy or overly animated flows.

## Design Principles

1. Make capture and review fast: primary actions should stay obvious without dominating the page.
2. Preserve scanability: lists, boards, filters, and dashboard cells should support quick comparison.
3. Keep the product quiet: visual energy should clarify state and priority, not decorate.
4. Prefer inline clarity: expose status, errors, and next actions close to the object they affect.
5. Respect local-first trust: the UI should feel stable, predictable, and under the user's control.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve visible focus states, reduced-motion compatibility, keyboard access for forms and controls, and color-independent priority/status cues wherever feasible.
